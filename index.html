What is Minimalism?

Minimalism is a design and lifestyle philosophy that focuses on simplicity, clarity, and functionality. It promotes the idea of removing excess elements to highlight what truly matters.

Why It Matters

By eliminating unnecessary distractions, minimalism helps create space for clarity — both visually and mentally. In design, this means clean layouts, intentional color use, and thoughtful typography.

How to Apply It

  • Use a limited color palette
  • Focus on essential elements
  • Prioritize readability and balance
  • Embrace whitespace
